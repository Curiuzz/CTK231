Christopher's Portfolio

Hi! My name is Christopher Posner and I'm an aspiring game designer. I am currently enrolled in the game design program at Illinois State University and I'm learning as much as I can about the game design process. I also enjoy writing poems every once in a while, and I figure that writing poems will help with writing stories for games in the future. Below is all of the projects I have worked on during my time in the game design program.

Rowed to Glory

Rowed to Glory is a linear, multiplayer board game in which your goal is to collect the most fish before everybody reaches the end.

Who's Texting Me?

Who's Texting Me? is a multiplayer party card game in which your goal is to play the funniest text message response to a text message prompt.
