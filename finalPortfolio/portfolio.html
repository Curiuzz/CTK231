Christopher's Portfolio

Hi! My name is Christopher Posner and I'm an aspiring game designer. I am currently enrolled in the game design program at Illinois State University and I'm learning as much as I can about the game design process. I also enjoy writing poems every once in a while, and I figure that writing poems will help with writing stories for games in the future. Below is all of the projects I have worked on during my time in the game design program.

Rowed to Glory

Rowed to Glory is a linear, multiplayer board game in which your goal is to collect the most fish before everybody reaches the end.

Who's Texting Me?

Who's Texting Me? is a multiplayer party card game in which your goal is to play the funniest text message response to a text message prompt.

The Book Game

The Book Game is a multiplayer board game in which your goal is to escape a haunted library by seeking out books, which give you hints on how to escape.

Charmoid Racing

Charmoid Racing is a racing video game in which there are 8 playable characters called Charmoids, and 4 playable tracks. Each Charmoid has their own special ability they can use while racing. While coming up with the idea for this game, I felt inspired to create audio tracks for each racetrack.

Stray Kitten

"Stray Kitten" is a poem I originally wrote during my sophomore year in high school and have since refined. This poem was inspired by a woman I knew who didn't like the way she looked, even though I knew she was beautiful.

The Ladder

"The Ladder" is a poem I recently wrote, and it very much resonates with me. This poem describes my regret for not feeling happier after my back surgery and getting my life back. I was very depressed in high school because of a crippling back injury, and while I've recovered from it physically, it's hard for me to recover from it mentally.
