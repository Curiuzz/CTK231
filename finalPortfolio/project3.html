The Book Game

The Book Game is a multiplayer board game in which your goal is to escape a haunted library by seeking out books, which give you hints on how to escape. There are 6 different color paths to choose from each game, all offering different bookmarks (powerups), hints, stories, and traps. Landing on book spaces allows you to read a book, which will give you either a bookmark, a hint, a story, or a trap. Reading hints and completing the tasks on them will eventually lead you to escape the library!
This game was inspired by a game that I made many times with my brother when we were younger, also titled The Book Game. In our version of the game, we would use actual books and lay them out on the floor of our house to create a large board of spaces, and we would use toys as characters to move across the spaces. Every time we made The Book Game, the layout was different, so it was a ton of fun. The game wasn't as fun for our parents, however, who had to avoid tripping on the books scattered across the house. I'm glad I was able to make this game in honor of the game I made with my brother when I was young.
