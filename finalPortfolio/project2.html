Who's Texting Me?

Who's Texting Me? is a multiplayer party card game in which your goal is to play the funniest text message response to a text message prompt. Each round, there will be a judge who reads a text message prompt to the group. The other players' goals are to play the funniest response to the prompt with the cards in their hand. Each player gets dealt 5 cards to start the game and draws a new card every time they play one, so they always have 5 cards. Response cards can be either emojis, text, or images.
